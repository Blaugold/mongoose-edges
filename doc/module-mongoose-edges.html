Module: mongoose-edges

mongoose-edges

Store edges and their properties between two objects.

Methods

<static> findEdges(query, cb)

Finds multiple edges meeting the criteria specified by query. src, dest and find are optional but at least one of them hast to exist in the query to yield any results. They specify the criteria for the query. props specifies which fields to return. The callback is either called with an error or an array of edges.
